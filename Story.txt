**Story**
Once upon a time, in a forest, there lived a monkey who resided on a berry tree, which was on the banks of a river.
In the forest lived a crocodile and his wife who wanted to eat the monkey's heart.
the monkey wanted to make a cloth for himself using crocodile skin. He slowly started trying to be friends with the
crocodile by feeding him the berries. The berries were delicious and the crocodile liked it. After sometime,
the monkey started poisoning the berries.
